Privacy Policy Last updated: May 2025 This Privacy Policy explains how the IRLMate app ("we", "our", "the app") handles user data. IRLMate is developed and published by jurianoff. The app aggregates chat and stream data from multiple streaming platforms to assist IRL streamers during live sessions. 1. Platforms and Services Covered IRLMate currently integrates or plans to integrate with the following services: Twitch Kick YouTube (planned) TikTok Live (planned) The app connects to these services solely to display public chat messages and stream statuses. 2. What Data We Collect IRLMate: Does not collect or store personal data from users by default. May store access tokens locally on your device to enable features like logged-in chat or stream status for Twitch/Kick. Does not share any data with third parties. 3. Permissions The app may request the following permissions: Internet access – required to fetch chat and stream data. Keep screen on – optional setting for usability during live streaming. No sensitive permissions such as camera, microphone, location, or storage are used. 4. Third-Party Services IRLMate uses public APIs and WebSocket/IRC endpoints provided by Twitch, Kick, and in future YouTube and TikTok. We do not control the privacy practices of those services. Please refer to their respective privacy policies: Twitch: https://www.twitch.tv/p/legal/privacy-notice/ Kick: https://kick.com/privacy YouTube: https://policies.google.com/privacy TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy 5. User Control Users can: Remove their login session at any time Revoke access from the streaming platforms they use Delete the app to remove all stored tokens and settings 6. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Updates will be posted publicly and versioned. For questions, contact: jurianoff via GitHub or the support contact listed in the Google Play Store.
