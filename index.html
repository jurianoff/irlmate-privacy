Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the IRLMate app ("we", "our", "the app") handles user data. IRLMate is developed and published by jurianoff. The app aggregates chat and stream data from multiple streaming platforms to assist IRL streamers during live sessions.

1. Platforms and Services Covered

IRLMate currently integrates or plans to integrate with:

  • Twitch
  • Kick
  • YouTube (planned)
  • TikTok Live (planned)

The app connects to these services solely to display public chat messages and stream statuses.

2. What Data We Collect

  • IRLMate does not collect or store personal data from users by default.
  • It may store access tokens locally to enable logged-in features.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

3. Permissions

  • Internet access – required for core functionality
  • Keep screen on – optional, for streaming usability

4. Third-Party Services

IRLMate connects to public APIs and chat systems from:

  • Twitch
  • Kick
  • YouTube
  • TikTok

5. User Control

Users can:

  • Remove login session at any time
  • Revoke API access from platform settings
  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this document. All changes will be published here. Contact: jurianoff
